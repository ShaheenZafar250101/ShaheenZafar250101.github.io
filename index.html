About Us

Dot is what creation begins with, and at Dot Animations, we take that idea to heart. Our passion for creativity and originality drives everything we do, and we love nothing more than helping our clients tell their stories in unforgettable ways. Why choose us? Because we're committed to delivering results. We start by conducting up-front research of your organization, competitors, target audience/market, and customer psychographics. Only after we fully understand you and your customers/clients do we recommend a plan that's tailored to your specific needs. Our team brings a diverse background of animation, motion graphics, advertising, design, branding, public relations, research, and strategic planning to work for your company. Not only will your videos look great, but they'll also get results. We're committed to providing cost-effective business solutions in minimal timelines while ensuring quality standards through effective communication and best practices. Whether you need a corporate video, explainer video, 2D or 3D animation, virtual reality video, or any other type of animated content, Dot Animations has the expertise and creativity to bring your ideas to life. We work closely with our clients every step of the way to ensure their message is unforgettable and their goals are achieved. Join our growing list of satisfied clients and contact us today to learn more about how Dot Animations can help you achieve your goals through the power of animation.
